Hero slider art for /transfer.

Expected filenames (all .webp, ~1600×640 px, <200KB):

  genesis-01.webp, genesis-02.webp, genesis-03.webp
  legacies-01.webp, legacies-02.webp
  newbies-01.webp, newbies-02.webp
  highlights-01.webp, highlights-02.webp, highlights-03.webp

Notes
- Keep focal subject roughly centered or slightly to the right; the left
  third of each slide is overlaid with a dark gradient to hold the title.
- Safe area for legibility: bottom ~40% of the image will be darkened.
- While files are absent, the slider renders a fallback gradient + label.
- To add a new slide, drop a file following the pattern {tab}-{n}.webp and
  add the entry in heroSlidesForTab() in src/pages/Transfer.tsx.
