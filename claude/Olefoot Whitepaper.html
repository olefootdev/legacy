3.3 · Core Product Features

Six systems. One product.

01 · Match Narrator

GameSpirit AI

Not a chatbot — the referee, commentator, and tactical advisor integrated frame-by-frame into the simulation loop. Resolves shots, builds story arcs, decides penalties. Powered by Anthropic Claude via Railway API.

02 · Tactical Geometry

SmartField

Python-generated zones, positional anchors, radius of influence. Exported as JSON, consumed by the match engine at zero runtime cost. Tactical intelligence is pre-computed — not generated live.

03 · Autonomous Players

Yuka Agents · 22 per match

Each player is an independent AI agent using Yuka steering behavior. They respond to ball position, team phase, opponent pressure, and individual DNA (riskTaking, buildUpPreference, pressIntensity). The field is alive.

04 · Career Tiers

Non-Purchasable Progression

Eight tiers of lifetime progression. EXP earned through matches — gols, assists, tactical decisions. Cannot be reset or purchased. Signature Moves unlock through attribute thresholds.

05 · Liquidity Engine

Market Maker

Instant buy offers for any player, with dynamic pricing based on position and rarity (20% discount for OVR 75+, 35% for lower rated). Every transaction in the public feed. The market never stops. Revenue never stops.

06 · Real Opponents

Asynchronous PvP

Matchmaking uses OVR differential of ±10 points against real managers' squads. Intelligent fallback ensures no match is ever without an opponent. Results generate EXP and League Points regardless of online status.

4.3 · The Architectural Decision

AI at creation, not at runtime.

Most AI-native games generate responses at runtime: every player action, every match event, every piece of narrative costs an API call. As the user base grows, so does the AI bill — often exponentially. This is the "AI scaling trap" that has broken several well-funded startups.

Olefoot inverts this model. Claude is used once per player creation — to generate the player's DNA: attributes, behavioral traits, personality, Signature Moves, narrative backstory. Once generated, this DNA is stored in Supabase and consumed by the match engine with no additional API calls.

Cost of AI per match played
$0
After initial player DNA generation
1 match vs 1,000,000 matches
Identical
Marginal cost is invariant — this is the architecture that scales.

5.1 · What Legacy Mode Is

A Ronaldinho card
that plays like Ronaldinho.

In Legacy Mode, any professional footballer can be transformed into a digital card with their own DNA — real attributes, career history, playing style. With the advancement of AI agents, each card now carries an autonomous agent that makes decisions inside the match based on the real player's profile.

A Ronaldinho in Legacy Mode is not just a card with 92 dribbling. It is an agent that prefers to receive with its back to goal, that activates pressingTrap when morale is high, that executes dummyRun at the right moments — because the agent was trained with that player's tactical DNA.

Appendix C · Glossary

Terms used in this whitepaper.

GameSpiritThe AI narrative engine that resolves match events (goals, penalties, red cards) and generates real-time commentary via Claude.
SmartFieldPython-generated tactical geometry: zones, positional anchors, radius of influence. Pre-computed and served as JSON — zero runtime AI cost.
Yuka AgentsIndependent steering-behavior AI agents for each of the 22 players on the field. Each agent reads DNA and field context to make autonomous decisions.
DNAPlayer behavioral profile generated once by Claude: attributes, traits (riskTaking, buildUpPreference), Signature Moves, and narrative backstory.
Legacy ModeThe system for transforming real professional players into digital cards with autonomous AI agents trained on their tactical DNA.
OLEXPThe on-chain token representing value within the Olefoot economy. Layer 2 / Solana-compatible. Roadmap Year 2 launch.
EXPLifetime, non-purchasable experience points that determine career tier progression for the manager.
Market MakerThe system that guarantees instant liquidity for player card sales by providing dynamic buy offers based on position and OVR rating.
OVROverall Rating — composite metric calculated from player attributes (pace, finishing, passing, tackling, vision, strength).
